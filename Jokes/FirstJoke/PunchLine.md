The punch-line is:

You need the following:
1. A saw
2. A can of peas
3. A can opener

The way you catch him is:
1. You cut a hole in the ice with the saw.
2. You open the can of peas.
3. You place the can of peas next to the hole in the ice.
4. When the bear bends over to take a pea, you kick him in the ice hole.
